About

Creative Journey

My artistic journey spans decades of drawing, painting, sculpting, and crafting—creative expression has always been my core passion. After years in accounting, I discovered web development offered everything I was seeking: remote flexibility, project-based work, and the freedom to work independently while continuously learning and growing.

Left Brain + Right Brain

What makes this transition particularly fulfilling is finally using my complete skill set. Throughout my life, I've excelled at both logical problem-solving and creative thinking, but was often told these were incompatible paths. Web development, especially CSS illustration, allows me to harness both strengths simultaneously—creating something deeply satisfying and authentic to who I am.

Global Perspective

My multicultural background—growing up with Swedish family roots while living across Sweden, the US, and now Canada for the past decade—brings a unique global perspective to my work. This diverse experience, combined with my multilingual abilities in English, Swedish, and Spanish, helps me approach design challenges with cultural sensitivity and broad creative influence.

Beyond The Screen

When I'm not coding, you'll find me with paint on my hands, sketching new concepts, or exploring three-dimensional forms through sculpture. Every creative medium informs my digital work, bringing organic flow and artistic intuition to the structured world of web development.
